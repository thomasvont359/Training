Where AI Is Good

Drafting & Rewriting

Emails, documents, responses

E.g., rewrite support email

Summarizing & Distilling

Long text into concise insights

E.g., TL;DR in 120 words

Extracting & Structuring

Information into tables and lists

E.g., extract fields A/B/C

Brainstorming

Generating variations and ideas

E.g., 10 subject line ideas

Repetitive Tasks

Handling clear rules and patterns efficiently

E.g., weekly invoice classification

AI excels when tasks are patternable, examples are provided, and success can be expressed as structure or criteria.
