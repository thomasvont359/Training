EU Essentials

Core considerations for AI work in the EU

Privacy (GDPR)

Process only necessary personal data; document lawful basis; honor data subject rights; prefer anonymization.

AI Act Readiness

Know your system’s risk class; keep tech docs; ensure transparency, human oversight, and data governance.

Security & Transfers

Use vetted vendors; apply SCCs/TIAs for non‑EU transfers; log access; least‑privilege keys and environments.

Transparency

Label AI assistance, disclose sources/limits, and provide user‑friendly explanations for decisions.
