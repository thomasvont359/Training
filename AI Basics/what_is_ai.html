What is AI?

A practical definition and how to think about it at work.

  • Working definition
    Software that learns patterns from data to make predictions, generate content, or take actions.
  • Common types
    Predictive models (classification/regression), generative models (text, images), retrieval and tool‑use agents.
  • Strengths & limits
    Excellent at patterned tasks and rapid drafting; needs oversight for facts, numbers, and novel edge cases.
