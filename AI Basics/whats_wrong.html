Weak Prompt

Why the right one wins

The strong prompt names the role, audience, timing, and pricing; sets length and tone; includes three pitching contexts and a required link; and ends with a clear call to action - so you get a ready-to-send email instead of a generic draft.

Strong Prompt
You're a senior product marketer. Draft a 200-word internal announcement email for our sales reps about the new "Insight Hub" analytics feature launching on 28 Oct. Explain what it does in one sentence, who it's for (mid-market accounts), when to pitch it (QBRs, renewal risk, expansion scouting), pricing (included in Pro+, $49 add-on for Standard), and include the one-pager link: /sales/insight-hub-onepager. Close with a clear call to action to book Friday's enablement session.
