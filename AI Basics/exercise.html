Hands-On Exercise

10 minutes

Task

Write the prompt that will make the AI draft a clear, empathetic reply to this customer complaint. You are not writing the email yourself, only the prompt that tells the AI what to produce.

Customer complaint

From: Alex Johnson, Order #48321 Subject: Late delivery & damaged box I finally got my order today, a week late, and the box was crushed. I needed this for a client demo and it’s useless now. I’m sick of chasing tracking updates. Either fix this today or refund me. This has been a waste of time.

Ideal Prompt

Act as a senior support lead for customer communications.
Your goal is to draft a clear, empathetic reply to Alex about the late delivery and damaged box.
Here’s the context: Order #48321; one-week delay; crushed packaging; offer a free express reship or a full refund plus a small credit.
Keep it under 160 words in plain English; acknowledge the issue and take ownership; present the two options and ask Alex to reply with his choice; include a signature.
Produce a ready-to-send email to Alex that ends with "reply with your choice".

A great prompt yields a reply that is empathetic, specific, and actionable with two clear remedies and a short next step.
