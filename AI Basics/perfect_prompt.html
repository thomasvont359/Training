The Perfect Prompt

A real‑world example with Role, Goal, Context, Constraints, and Output

Role

Act as the Senior Product Manager serving enterprise customers. You understand buyer needs, can weigh trade-offs, and are empowered to propose realistic dates.

Goal

Write a short customer email that announces the deprecation of Feature X and guides customers to migrate to Y.

Context

Feature X has ~8% usage and is expensive to run. Feature Y is GA and includes SSO and audit logs. A typical migration takes about 2 hours per tenant. Finance requires two weeks’ notice and SOC 2 audit logs.

Constraints
  • Sound respectful and confident.
  • Keep it under 120 words.
  • State the cutoff date, admin steps, and a support link.
  • Don’t mention or imply unreleased items.
Output

Return a ready-to-send customer email: a subject line, 2–3 short paragraphs, and a concise bullet list of next steps. End with a support contact.

Tip: Keep each section explicit in your draft prompt, then remove labels if you prefer a cleaner final prompt.
